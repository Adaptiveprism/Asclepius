Compilation of Eastern Healing Practices:

1. Sikh Kriyas https://yadi.sk/i/EjHgQ3gUdmWY9
2. Hindu Yoga https://yadi.sk/i/0zmCrfTBdmWWu
3. Bhuddist Meditation https://i.imgur.com/WuvekpE.jpg https://raw.githubusercontent.com/Adaptiveprism/Asclepius/master/Bhuddist%20Meditation.txt
4. Tibetan Breathing Exercises http://www.scribd.com/doc/32476168/The-Ten-Tibetan-Breaths
5. Taoist Tai Chi http://vimeo.com/14250730

I recommend a daily practice. This is essential for discipline. Zeal and intensity throughout each exercise is required.

It should take you approximately an hour to complete one of the above, with the exception of the Tai Chi.

After the Kriya, Yoga, and Tibetan Breaths lay in Shavasana for 10-20 minutes so the nervous system can integrate the changes. https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Shavasana
